How to Play

1
Challenge a Friend
Create a game and send the link. One click to join.
2
Pick Your Secret
Choose a number and lock it in. No take-backs.
3
Guess & React
Take turns guessing. Your opponent tells you Higher, Lower, or Correct! — and you can see their range narrowing in real time.
4
Win the Clash
First to crack the code wins! Run out of guesses? Closest guess takes it.
Timer: 12 seconds per turn. Too slow? Your opponent steals the turn.
Difficulty: Easy (1-50), Medium (1-100), Hard (1-500) — each with a guess limit.
Hints: Get a “Very close!” alert when you’re breathing down their number.
Tie? If both players run out of guesses, the closest guess wins.
